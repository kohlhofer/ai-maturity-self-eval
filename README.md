# How are you actually using AI?

A two-minute self-assessment of AI usage maturity.

Everyone thinks they're using AI fully. In practice everyone uses it differently, and most people rate themselves higher than they are, because frequency feels like maturity. It isn't.

The assessment places you on two axes:

- **Capability** — how many of the things AI can do for you (connect to your systems, hold persistent context, run multi-stage workflows, close feedback loops, work semi-autonomously) you actually practise, and how deeply.
- **Impact** — whether your AI work has actually landed: shipped, used by others, a number you checked that moved.

Those two place you in one of four quadrants: Dabbler, Sharp operator, Output factory, or Force multiplier.
