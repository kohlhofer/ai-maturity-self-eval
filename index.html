The AI Leverage Check · a 2-minute self-check

Everyone thinks they're using AI fully.

They're not. Everyone uses it differently, and most people rate themselves higher than they are, because frequency feels like leverage. It isn't.

Two things make the difference: how many of the things AI can do for you that you actually practise, and whether your AI work has actually landed — shipped, used by others, a number that moved. This rates both, on what you've genuinely done. Two minutes, three steps.
